# 📊 Procurement Category Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of procurement performance across different purchasing channels. It enables procurement managers and business stakeholders to compare revenue, cost, and profitability across **ADHOC**, **ARC (Annual Rate Contract)**, and **Routed Orders** while identifying the most profitable product industries and clients.

The dashboard was designed to simplify procurement decision-making by providing interactive filtering, financial KPIs, and comparative analysis in a single report.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

Organizations often procure materials through multiple procurement methods, making it difficult to compare their financial performance.

This dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- Which procurement method generates the highest profit?
- Which product industries are most profitable?
- Which clients contribute the highest revenue?
- How do Gross Margin, Sales, and COGS compare across procurement channels?
- Which procurement strategy should be preferred based on profitability?

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Executive KPIs

- 💰 Total Revenue
- 📊 Overall Gross Margin %

---

### Interactive Filters

- Date Range
- Brand Selection

---

### Product Industry Analysis

Compare Gross Margin across procurement methods:

- ADHOC
- ARC
- ADHOC + ARC (Routed)

This helps identify industries where procurement performs better under specific purchasing methods.

---

### Client Performance Analysis

Client-wise comparison including:

- COGS
- Sales
- Gross Margin

for

- ADHOC Procurement
- ARC Procurement
- Routed Procurement

This enables quick identification of high-value and low-margin customers.
